    Cancellation Policy

    All monies will be refunded in full (less a $50 processing fee) should you cancel 30 or more days before your program’s start date. If you cancel between 11 and 29 days before the start of your program a non-refundable credit voucher (less a $50 processing fee) will be issued which can be used towards any other group program within one year from the date of issue. If you cancel between 1-10 days before the start of your program a non-refundable credit voucher (less a $100 processing fee) will be issued which can be used towards any other group program within one year from the date of issue. No credit or refund is available if you arrive late, leave early for any reason, or if you do not show up. The Option Institute reserves the right to cancel any program with a 14-day notice in which case you will be given a full refund of tuition paid.
