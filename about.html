Micholyn Gayoso - Psychologist

Dr. Gayoso is a licensed psychologist who earned her Doctorate in Clinical Psychology from the Texas School of Professional Psychology.

Dr. Gayoso has extensive experience in a variety of Evaluations, testing for: Autism, ADHD, Learning Disabilities, Mood/Personality Disturbance, Psychiatric Diagnostic Considerations, Developmental Delays, & Neuropsychological functioning. It is her belief that proper and accurate testing can ensure the most appropriate direction for treatment.
